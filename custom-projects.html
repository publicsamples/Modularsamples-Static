Custom Projects

I’ve spent years working with sound. As a musicain, through public domain sample libraries and now with plugin development. My focus is on the more unusual side of things: experimental tools, esoteric effects, and ideas that don’t fit neatly into the mainstream.

If you have a concept for a plugin but aren’t sure how to bring it to life, I can help with development, consulting, or just refining the idea into something workable. Whether it’s an offbeat effect, a left-field instrument, or something entirely unclassifiable, I’m always interested in projects that explore new ground.

If that sounds like what you need, feel free to get in touch.
