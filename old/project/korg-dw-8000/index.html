Korg DW-8000

The Korg DW-8000 is a vintage hybrid synthesizer renowned for its rich and versatile sound palette. Combining digital waveforms with analog filters and envelopes, it offers musicians a wide range of sonic possibilities. Released in the late 1980s, the DW-8000 features eight voices of polyphony and 16 oscillators, providing ample room for lush pads, vibrant leads, and complex textures. Its distinctive sound is characterized by its warm analog filters and digital waveforms.
