Oi, Grandad!

Oi, Grandad! is a four voice granular synth for MacOS VST & AU and Windows VST.

It has independent controls for each voice, 12 modulation sequencers and the ability to use any of the voices as a mod source. It is capable of being played using MIDI or you can trigger all four voices at the push of a button. Currently there are two versions available, a more stable v1 branch and a flakier, but more feature pack v2.

Additionally, a large collection of extra content for Oi, Grandad! is available to purchase. It includes:

  • Over 3gb of loops, stems drones and more
  • 60+ unique presets
  • Brings grandad back to life

Features

  • 4 independant granular voices with extensive controls
  • 12 indepentantly triggered complex modulation sources
  • Tracking modulation
  • Drone button for sustained playback
  • Delay per voice
  • Round Robin mode
  • Crossfade mode
