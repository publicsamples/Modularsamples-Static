Additionally there are 4 LFOs, 2 envelopes, a pair of modulation sequencers & a gate sequencer.</p><img src="http://default/sites/default/files/inline-images/ms30.jpg" data-entity-uuid="38e1a90e-0107-4c03-b0ba-706a451a1c57" data-entity-type="file" alt="ms-30" class="align-center" width="500" height="363" loading="lazy" /><p> </p><h4><strong>MS-40</strong></h4><p>MS-40 features four stacked synth voices that together create massive, layered sounds.</p><img src="http://default/sites/default/files/inline-images/ms40_0.jpg" data-entity-uuid="d74695ce-4a21-4b73-8b97-984b4772a3ce" data-entity-type="file" alt="ms-40" class="align-center" width="500" height="374" loading="lazy" /><p> </p><h4><strong>MS-4v</strong></h4><p>An adapted version of MS-40. MS-4V use the round robin technique to cycle between 4 voices with each new note played. This gives a similar effect to that found on synths like the Korg Monopoly, or Oberheim Four Voice.</p><img src="http://default/sites/default/files/inline-images/ms4v%20copy.jpg" data-entity-uuid="46b68a60-20a3-4c73-9fca-3da00316c696" data-entity-type="file" alt="ms-4v" class="align-center" width="500" height="374" loading="lazy" /><p> </p><p><strong>MS-DR</strong></p><p>Another adaptation on MS-40, MS-DR makes the voices available on single keys and is designed from drum and percussion work.</p><img src="http://default/sites/default/files/inline-images/msdr%20copy.jpg" data-entity-uuid="9826e10d-2a39-4094-bd1e-a88267ed9d7a" data-entity-type="file" alt="ms-dr" class="align-center" width="500" height="374" loading="lazy" /><p> </p><h4><strong>MS-TM</strong></h4><p>MS-TM uses Konakt's granular "Tone Machine" to give a different flavour to the libraries, great at strings, evolving pads and odd noises.
